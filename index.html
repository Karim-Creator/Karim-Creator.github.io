About Us

Since our start in 1976, we have made over 500,000 pets happy with the addition of a pet door to their home. We know more about dog and cat than anyone else in the world, and we love using our knowledge to help people find the perfect pet products.

Whether your dog needs a long walk, a jog, a brisk run, or to simply get out in the yard to relieve themselves, we are here to help. We are there for your dog when you can’t be.
